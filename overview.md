# Good Finances – Overview

## What is Good Finances?

Good Finances is a simple personal finance toolkit designed for people who want to feel in control of their money without becoming full-time finance nerds.

It focuses on:
- **Clarity** – one place to see income, fixed costs, goals, and debt.
- **Calm** – step-by-step prompts instead of complicated dashboards.
- **UK reality** – aligned to UK tax years, ISAs, pensions, student loans.
- **Accessibility** – dyslexia-aware design and low-friction flows.

## Who it’s for

- Young professionals in the UK figuring out savings, ISAs, and pensions.  
- People who feel “behind” with money and want a reset.  
- Neurodivergent users (including dyslexic thinkers) who need clean, low-noise layouts.

## Core components

- Private **Good Finances AI assistant** (not public yet).  
- Budget + bill planners.  
- Savings goals & sinking funds trackers.  
- Pension / ISA planning templates.  
- Simple “money calendar” for the year.

This repo describes the product and direction; implementation details live elsewhere.
